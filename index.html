About me

Thank you for visiting my page and taking a moment to learn a little about me.

Who am I: I am person who pushes myself beyond my own expectations to discover new frontiers. I am motivated to learn and grow in order to help myself and loved ones as best I can. I am the oldest in my family out of 4 siblings(1 brother and 2 sisters). I was born in Puebla,Puebla, Mexico and brought to San Bernardino, CA, USA at the age of 6. I am a Dreamer who is currently part of the Deffered Action for Childhood Arrivals, DACA. I focus on what is within my capability to change and don't worry to much about what is beyond my control. However, I do my best to prepare for the unexpected by having different game plans just in case my original one does not work.

What I Enjoy: I like to fail fast and often to learn from my mistakes. Its not that i like to fail, rather its a step closer to learing something new and being good at it. I have always liked to learn how things work and can't help myself from taking things apart to figure out how they work. Beside, taring things apart i enjoy spending quality time with my family. My favorite thing to do is to try something new such as skydiving, snowboarding, surfing or just a different type of food and then bring others along to experience what I have enjoyed.

What I Don't like: I don't like to be micromanaged. I don't like to wait in long lines. Other than that I don't complain too much.

What I have Achived: First of all I am alive and well. I graduated high school. I am not in jail. I am not a teen parent. I am drug-free. Believe it or not, many old friends and family can't say the same. I have an A.A. in: Physics, Mathamatics, Chemisty. I have a B.S. in Mechanical Engineer. I am a homeowner of two homes, best thing is they pay for themselves. I have paid of my first car and debt free(beside mortgages).
